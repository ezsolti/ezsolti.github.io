blocks.Collimator()¶

class feign.blocks.Collimator(*args, **kwargs)¶

A class used to represent a Collimator. Any gamma ray not passing through the Collimator will be rejected. Collimators have an impact only if they are attributed to Detector objects with Detector.set_collimator(). The front and the back of the collimator cannot intersect.
